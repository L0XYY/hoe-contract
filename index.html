Welcome to Hall Of Evolutions

Please carefully read the Independent Contractor Agreement below.

1. Deadlines & Communication

  • The Developer agrees to complete assigned tasks by agreed-upon deadlines.
  • Failure to complete tasks by deadlines, or repeated missed deadlines, may result in immediate removal from the project and forfeiture of any revenue percentage or position.
  • Inactivity without prior notice for extended periods, or poor communication with the project lead, may result in removal from the team.

2. Compensation & Adjustments

  • Payment terms will be established before work begins.
  • Hall Of Evolutions reserves the right to transition compensation from percentage-based to task-based payment if deemed necessary for the project's health.

3. Intellectual Property & Theft

  • All work created for the project remains the exclusive property of Hall Of Evolutions.
  • Any attempt to steal, copy, or reuse project assets, code, or systems without explicit permission is strictly prohibited.
  • Removed team members may not retain ownership or copies of project assets, systems, or files.

4. Confidentiality & Leaks

  • Sharing, leaking, or distributing project files, scripts, assets, or internal information is prohibited.
  • Failure to maintain strict confidentiality regarding the project may result in immediate removal and potential blacklisting.

5. Conduct & Termination

  • Toxic behavior, harassment, or abuse toward team members will not be tolerated and may result in immediate termination.
  • Any actions that damage, jeopardize, or negatively affect the project or its reputation may result in immediate removal.
  • Hall Of Evolutions reserves the right to remove the contractor at any time if they are deemed unfit for the role.
